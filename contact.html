FAQ

Quick Answers

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard 5-page website takes 2–4 weeks. E-commerce stores take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. We always provide a clear timeline upfront.

Do you work with small businesses and startups?

Absolutely. We work with everyone from early-stage startups to established enterprises. We have packages for every stage of growth.

What's included in the free consultation?

A 30-minute call where we analyse your current digital presence, discuss your goals, and outline a clear path forward. No obligations whatsoever.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes — we offer monthly retainer packages for maintenance, updates, SEO, and continued marketing. We're a long-term growth partner, not a one-off vendor.

How do you handle payments?

We typically work on a 50% upfront, 50% on completion model. For larger projects and retainers, we offer milestone-based payment structures.
